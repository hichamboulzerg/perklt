Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how perklt.com ("we", "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you visit or interact with our website.

1. Information we collect

When you visit this site or click a reward offer, we automatically collect:

  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URL, pages visited, timestamps.
  • Approximate geographic data: country, region, and city derived from your IP.
  • Click data: source and campaign identifiers from the URL you arrived on.

We do not request or store payment information, and we do not require an account to browse the site.

2. How we use information

  • To measure site performance and traffic sources.
  • To deliver the offers described on the site.
  • To prevent abuse, bot traffic, and fraudulent conversions.
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations.

3. Third parties

Clicking a reward offer redirects you to our partner OGAds (CPAGrip LLC / similar CPA networks), who operate independent offer pages. Their collection and use of your information is governed by their own privacy policies. We also rely on Cloudflare for DNS, caching, and bot protection; Cloudflare may log request metadata as described in their policy.

4. Cookies and similar technologies

We use minimal first-party identifiers (a short random click ID) to attribute conversions back to the source that sent a visitor. We do not use advertising cookies directly on this site.

5. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you, and to object to certain uses. To make a request, email the contact address on the Contact page.

6. Children

This site is intended for users 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18.

7. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
